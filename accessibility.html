Accessibility Statement

Last updated: 13 October 2025

♿

Our Commitment

Pinched is committed to ensuring that our app and services are accessible to as many people as possible.

We design Pinched with accessibility in mind, aiming to support features such as text scaling, screen readers, and clear colour contrasts. We follow recognised accessibility guidelines where possible and continuously improve based on feedback.

🔤 Text Scaling
📢 Screen Reader Support
🎨 Clear Colour Contrast
📱 Cross-device Support
🔧

Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. As Pinched develops, we are working to expand support for additional accessibility features across devices.

We review our app regularly and welcome input from users to help us identify and fix barriers as quickly as possible.

💬

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier while using Pinched, please let us know. Your feedback helps us make Pinched better for everyone.

✉️ support@pinchedapp.com
💡 We aim to respond to all accessibility feedback within 48 hours and will work to address issues as a priority.
